Install the TradingView bookmarklet
Do this once — then click it anytime on a TradingView chart to instantly read your cloud values.
1

☁ Read Ripster Clouds
If dragging doesn't work, right-click → "Add to bookmarks" and paste the URL manually.
2
Go to your TradingView chart with the Ripster clouds visible. Make sure the right-side price axis is showing the EMA labels (the colored boxes).
3
Click the bookmarklet. It reads the cloud values from the page and copies them to your clipboard. You'll see a green toast notification confirming success.
4
Come back here and click "Paste from TradingView" below. Fields auto-fill and the trade plan calculates immediately.
